# Creative Agency Service Performance Analysis
Data Analytics Case Study | Excel | Power BI | Simulated Business Dataset

## Overview
This project evaluates the operational and financial performance of services offered by a simulated creative agency. Using a dataset of 100 projects, the analysis examines revenue patterns, cost structures, and service profitability to identify opportunities for strategic optimisation.

The goal is to demonstrate how data-driven decision-making can support service portfolio management within creative and digital agencies.

## Business Context
Creative agencies typically deliver a mix of strategic services (such as branding and consulting) and production services (such as videography, photography, and event coverage). While these services contribute to revenue, they often vary significantly in terms of operational cost, scalability, and profit margins.

This analysis explores how agencies can better understand the economic performance of each service line.

## Business Question
How can a creative agency optimise its service portfolio to maximise profitability while maintaining operational efficiency?

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (data structuring and preliminary analysis)
- Power BI (dashboard visualisation and business insights)

## Methodology
The analysis follows a structured approach:
- Data preparation and cleaning in Excel
- Exploratory analysis of revenue and cost distributions
- Profitability analysis across service categories
- Efficiency evaluation based on team size and project duration
- Visualisation and insight generation using Power BI

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains 100 simulated agency projects and includes the following variables:
- Client industry
- Service type
- Project revenue
- Operational cost
- Team size
- Project duration

The dataset was designed to reflect realistic operational patterns commonly observed in creative service businesses.

## Analytical Framework
The analysis focuses on several key performance dimensions:

**Revenue Performance**
- Revenue distribution by service type
- Revenue concentration by client industry

**Profitability**
- Profit margin by service
- Service profitability index

**Operational Efficiency**
- Revenue per team member
- Project duration efficiency

**Strategic Portfolio Assessment**
- Comparison between strategic services and production services
- Identification of scalable service categories

## Key Insights
- Strategic services such as Branding and Strategy Consulting demonstrate the highest profit margins.
- Production services such as Event Coverage and Videography generate strong revenue but involve higher operational costs.
- Clients within the technology and consulting sectors contribute disproportionately to overall revenue.
- Strategic service offerings show greater scalability due to lower resource intensity.

## Strategic Implications
The analysis suggests that creative agencies can improve profitability by prioritising high-margin strategic services while maintaining production services as complementary revenue streams.

A balanced service portfolio that combines scalable consulting services with selective production offerings can support both revenue growth and operational sustainability.
